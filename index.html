Students Startups

An accelerator program for international students to gain real world experience

We understand… It is a big challenge for international students to find a professional job in Australia, even an internship or work experience.

International student Employability Accelerator (ISEA) is a five weeks curricula program for international students to work on real-life projects for startups or small business and engage in an active learning environment with startup enthusiast, entrepreneurs and innovators. It has the same credibility as working as an intern!

Unlock your next career opportunity

Not everyone wants to be an entrepreneur, but as future of work forecasts have predicted, we will all have to have to develop entrepreneurial skill sets. We believe you will be more employable and entrepreneurial after the five weeks program.

In the end of the program, we will make sure all your effort would be well presented on your CV and LinkedIn portfolio. You may also have an interview opportunity for a paid internship.
